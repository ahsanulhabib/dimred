<h4>Oil Data</h4>

<p>The results for the oil data with the density network can be recovered using <code>demOilDnet4</code> and <code>demOilDnet5</code>. They 100 samples and 400 samples respectively. Results are shown below.

<p><center><img src="demOilDnet4.png" width="49%"><img src="demOilDnet5.png" width="49%"><br>
<i>Left</i>: Visualisation of oil data with density networks and 100 samples from the latent distribution, <code>demOilDnet4</code>. Nearest neighbour classification in the latent space leads to 22 errors. <i>Right</i>: Visualisation of oil data with density network and 400 samples from the latent distribution, <code>demOilDnet5</code>. Nearest neighbour classification in the latent space leads to 16 errors.</center>


<p>The results for the oil data with the GTM can be recovered using <code>demOilDnet1</code>, <code>demOilDnet2</code> and <code>demOilDnet3</code>. They used grids of 10x10, 20x20 and 30x30 respectively. Results for 10x10 and 30x30 grids are shown below.

<p><center><img src="demOilDnet1.png" width="49%"><img src="demOilDnet3.png" width="49%"><br>
<i>Left</i>: Visualisation of oil data with GTM and a 10x10 grid, <code>demOilDnet1</code>. Nearest neighbour classification in the latent space leads to 74 errors. <i>Right</i>: Visualisation of oil data with GTM and a 30x30 grid, <code>demOilDnet3</code>. Nearest neighbour classification in the latent space leads to 11 errors.</center>
